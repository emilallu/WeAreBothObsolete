We Are
Both Obsolete

In a world obsessed with the newest, fastest, and shiniest technology, what happens to yesterday’s innovation? As we upgrade our devices, we simultaneously render ourselves outdated.

We Are Both Obsolete explores the unseen graveyard of our digital progress – where discarded hardware meets forgotten software in an ever-accelerating cycle of consumption and disposal.
